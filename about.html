About BMW

BMW is not just a car company — it is a symbol of performance, luxury and innovation. For more than a century, BMW has built machines that connect drivers with the road and their emotions.

Who We Are

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) was founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany. The company started by building aircraft engines during World War I. After the war, BMW shifted to motorcycles and later entered the automobile industry.

Over the years, BMW became known for creating vehicles that perfectly balance power, comfort, handling and design. Today, BMW is one of the world’s most respected premium automotive brands.

Evolution Over Time

  • 1916 Company founded in Munich.
  • 1923 Rolled out the first BMW motorcycle.
  • 1933 Manufactured the first BMW car.
  • 1972 BMW M Division created for motorsport dominance.
  • 1998 Rolls-Royce Motor Cars acquired.
  • 2013 BMW i electric brand launched.
  • Today Leading the world in luxury and electric mobility.

BMW Today

Today BMW builds petrol, diesel, hybrid and electric vehicles. Models like the i4, i7 and iX show BMW’s future in electric mobility. The company also works on autonomous driving, AI systems and sustainable production.

BMW continues to lead motorsports through its M Division, bringing racing technology directly to road cars.
