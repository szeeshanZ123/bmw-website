Practice Website — BMW Themed — 2026

BMW’s philosophy is captured in its slogan “The Ultimate Driving Machine.” Every BMW is built to provide sheer driving pleasure through powerful engines, precise handling, advanced safety systems and luxurious interiors.

Today, BMW Group owns the brands BMW, MINI and Rolls-Royce Motor Cars and operates in more than 140 countries worldwide. BMW also has strong roots in motorsport, where its BMW M division develops high-performance sports cars inspired by racing technology.

BMW is now moving toward the future with electric mobility through models like the BMW i4, i7 and BMW iX. The company continues to invest in autonomous driving, AI, connectivity and sustainability to create environmentally friendly vehicles without losing performance.

From aircraft engines to advanced electric cars, BMW has evolved into a global symbol of innovation, performance and luxury — always staying true to its promise of sheer driving pleasure.

Milestones

  • 1916 — Company founded in Munich
  • 1923 — First BMW motorcycle produced
  • 1933 — First BMW automobile launched
  • 1972 — BMW Motorsport (BMW M) founded
  • 1998 — Rolls-Royce Motor Cars acquired
  • 2013 — Electric BMW i brand launched
